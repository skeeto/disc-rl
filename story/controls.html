Pressing shift with a direction will move you in that direction until either you hit a wall or see a monster.

Attacking

Walking into an enemy performs a melee attack. You can also throw your identity disc at it, which will return to your hand.

  • w wait one turn
  • W wait in place until an enemy appears
  • f enters ranged attack mode / attacks the selected target
  • h/l select next/prev target for ranged attack

Exploration

  • c cancel current action (autoexplore, wait, ranged mode)
  • </> ascend/descend a bus
  • o autoexplore the level
  • ,/. automatically travel to up/down bus
